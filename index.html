Tips to Make the Most of Your Dumpster Rental

To help you get the best value from your dumpster rental, here are a few simple tips we recommend to all our customers:
Know Your Waste Type: Not everything can go into a dumpster (e.g., hazardous materials). Let us know what you’re disposing of so we can recommend the right solution.

  • Choose the Right Size: We’re happy to walk you through size options so you’re not paying for unused space—or running out too soon.
  • Don’t Overfill: For safety and compliance reasons, waste should not exceed the top edge of the dumpster.
  • Plan Your Placement: Consider where the dumpster will be dropped off. Ideally, choose a location with easy access for loading and pick-up.

We’re always happy to offer guidance before and during your rental to help you get the most from the experience.
